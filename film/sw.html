SWITCHSIDE (2023)

Putri lives with her disabled older brother and her abusive, gambling-addicted father. The constant violence and pressure at home gradually affect her mental state. Without realizing it, she develops schizophrenia and ultimately ends up killing her own family.

Directed by Ilham

Cinematography Rajiv

Editor Ilham

Produced by Jaya Abadi

Art Director Zainal Ferdiansyah

Length — 19 Minutes

Shot on Sony FX3
