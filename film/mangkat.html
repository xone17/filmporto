MANGKAT (2025)

Rian, a college student from Lampung, travels to Bandung with three other passengers in a shuttle car. Along the way, tensions rise when the car breaks down. In the midst of the situation, they begin to open up about their experiences of leaving home, their different backgrounds, and their individual perspectives while trying to find a way to continue the journey.

Directed by Zainal Ferdiansyah

Cinematography Lazuardi Fajar Ramadhan

Editor Atha Sahila

Produced by All of Us

Gaffer Irwan Sayid Hudaya

Length — 19 Minutes

Shot on Sony FX3
