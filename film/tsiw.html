THE SHADOW I WROTE (2025)

Alex (22) lives in a rented house and struggles with PTSD. One restless afternoon, he begins writing a book titled The Shadow I Wrote, hoping to make sense of his life. To his shock, Aldo (22), a character in the book, exists in reality trapped in a time loop and facing the same psychological struggles as Alex. As Alex tries to understand who controls their lives, he plans a confrontation with Aldo, believing that eliminating him could finally resolve the turmoil in his own mind.

Directed by Firhand Ardhana Ridwan

Cinematography Ahmad Raeka

2nd Assistant Director Zainal Ferdiansyah

Produced by Terus Terang Mas

Gaffer Irwan Sayid Hudaya

Length — 19 Minutes
