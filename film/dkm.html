DAKWAH KUDU MONETIZE (2025)

Haji Sodikin has recently been appointed as the head of the DKM (Mosque Prosperity Council). The mosque has grown increasingly quiet, and few congregants attend. To attract young people and revitalize the mosque, Sodikin launches a "Digital Dakwah" program. However, his desire for viral success spirals out of control he becomes consumed with social media management and selling goods using mosque facilities and congregants’ welfare funds. Faced with rumors of fund misuse, Sodikin must decide whether to restore his reputation or be replaced by a more responsible leader.
