DEDEN SAMBAS (2024)

Deden Sambas is a sculptor and contemporary artist from Bandung. Born and raised in Garut, he moved to Bandung during his teenage years, where he studied art independently at Sanggar Olah Seni Bandung. Known for his critical perspective and creative freedom, Deden is planning an unconventional outdoor exhibition a personal showcase that brings together his closest friends and collaborators.
