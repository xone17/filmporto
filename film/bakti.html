MANGKAT (2025)

Bakti is a mischievous young boy who spends most of his time playing games and rarely listens to his parents. One day, his pregnant mother falls after one of his careless acts. The incident becomes a turning point for Bakti, making him realize the importance of changing his behavior and helping his mother.

Directed by Saputra Tama

Cinematography Putra Erikson

Editor Saputra Tama

Art Director Zainal Ferdiansyah

Length — 10 Minutes

Shot on Sony A3 iii
